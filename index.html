The HCI Laboratory was founded in 2002 by prof. Jiří Sochor.
The aim was to research on new aspects and modalities of human-machine interfaces.
Today we closely collaborate with Visitlab and our research focuses on human-computer interaction, user experience, games user research, game design, esports, visualization, virtual reality, augmented reality and mixed reality.
Apart from research activities, we also teach multiple courses related to game design and development, HCI, computer graphics and visualizations.
